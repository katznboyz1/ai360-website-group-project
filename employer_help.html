According to the U.S. Department of Labor, not only are 8 out of 10 people with any kind of disability underemployed, but
they also are more likely to secure part-time positions and work in service occupations.

The Summit
The AI360 initiative will culminate in the two-day Ability and Inclusion Summit on November 1-2, 2018. The first day will offer
panels and workshops for employers. The second day will be a job fair, allowing qualified candidates with disabilities to initiate
the conversation with participating employers. The summit will include an AI360 "Shark Tank" Pitch Project. This project will provide
a forum for non-profits and start-ups supporting individuals with disabilities to pitch their program for funding and/or professional
support throughout the year.

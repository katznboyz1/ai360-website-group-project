If you identify as having an intellectual or developmental disability, like autism or cerebral palsy, you may face
challenges finding competitive employment. Hiring managers might overlook your resume, or might not be sensitive to your needs
during the interviewing process.If you've landed a position, perhaps you don't have the support you were looking for. We welcome you
to apply to a position within the AI360 community. Our valued partners have built a Diversity and Inclusion infrastructure within their
companies to reach qualified candidates like you. These partners are willing to listen and adapt to open up more opportunities to
candidates who might need a little more customized support or accommodations.
